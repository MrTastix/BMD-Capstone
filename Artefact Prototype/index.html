While the internet can be a great place to engage in communities to share experiences and learn new things, the internet is not a safe space.

We all desire to be treated with respect, but there are still large portions of online platforms where some individuals go against this, spreading hate and harassment towards others.

Despite cyberbullying being illegal, one third of NZ teens experience it everyday.

Cyberbullying is harassment and is not okay. If you or someone you know has experienced cyberbullying, help is available.
